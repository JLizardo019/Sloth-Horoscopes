The Disappointed Sloth

Capricorn


  • Dates: December 22 - January 19
  • Compatibility: Taurus, Cancer
  • Element: Earth
  • Likes: Family, tradition, music, understated status, quality craftsmanship
  • Dislikes: Almost everything at some point

Capricorn, you can be a bit of a know it all and you often get disappointed when other sloths do not meet your expectations. You are very responsible and hard-working which brings you lots of success in your personal and career life. Your respect for family and tradition is admirable. Sometimes your peers think you are cold, but in reality you are a big softie at heart. Don't be afraid to let your guard down and show your vulnerable side.
